Our Story

Bountiful Foods is a long time food and nutrition store in Carlsbad, California and was started by the current owner and operator, Sally Borenstein. The first location was in 1983 in her home and then the next year, moved to its current location.


Bountiful Foods started selling organic fruits and vegetables and recently has opened their doors to grass-fed meat and dairy as well as baked products.


“Our customers love that we have an organic selection for them,” said Sally.


Bountiful Foods sells many organic foods including: dairy products, meat, cheese, beans, fruits, nuts, organic potatoes, deli meat, eggs, fruits, organic pastas, organic brown rice and organic rice.


Bountiful Foods does not sell genetically modified foods.


Bountiful Foods was one of the three original businesses to initiate the San Diego County market hosted in the town plaza on a monthly basis since 1982.
